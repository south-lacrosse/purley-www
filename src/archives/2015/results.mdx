## Purley Win Play-off to Stay in Premiership

Since Purley finished in 9th in the Premiership for the season, we could
therefore be challenged for our Premiership spot by the losers of East v
West play-off. Purley won that [match against Maidstone by
10-7](maidstone-playoff-report), and therefore Purley will be playing top
level lacrosse again in 2015-16.

## A Team woes

Unfortunately the A team experiment never really got off the ground, and
after a win in week 1 the numbers, or organisation, were just never there.
We'll try again in a year or two, hopefully with more success.

## Purley Stay in Premiership

Even though the team finished last in the Premiership for the 2013-14
season, the winners of both East One and West One declined promotion to the
top division, preferring instead to to consolidate their position with an
extra season in the lower division. Therefore Purley will be playing top
level lacrosse again in 2014-15.

---
title: Dave Copple makes Wales Squad for 2012 Euros
date: 2012-06-14
---

Congratulations to Purley's Captain Dave Copple on making the Wales squad
for the 2012 European Championships. Wales came 10th after victories
against France, Norway, Slovakia (x2) and Switzerland, but narrowly missed
out on the 1/4 finals by a goal against Israel (are they really in Europe
anyway?), and also lost against Finland and Czech Republic.

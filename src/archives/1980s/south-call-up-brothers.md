---
title: South Call Up Brothers
date: 1981-04-24
showDate: true
byline: From the Croydon Advertiser
---

Two brothers from Purley Lacrosse Club are included in the 13 strong Under 16
squad representing the South against the North at Orpington Sports Club
tomorrow (Saturday, 1:30).

Andrew Slaughter, who attends Trinity School and reached his 16th birthday last
week, plays a midfield role and David, at Riddlesdown High School and a year
younger, is in attack.

They started playing lacrosse when their club decided to start a junior team
as a source of talent for the three senior sides they field regularly each week.

Purley, whose home ground is at Addiscombe Cricket Club, also have Ken
Mingard, the reserve goalkeeper, and Colin Atkinson, first reserve
defenceman, in the squad.

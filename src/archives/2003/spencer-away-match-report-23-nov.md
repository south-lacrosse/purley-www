title: Spencer Away
titleShort: 23 Nov Spencer
date: 2002-11-23
category: match-report
---

After heavy rain during the week, the Spencer pitch was covered with
copious amounts of surface water. Conditions like these can cause a
disjointed game, and reduce the effect skill has on the outcome the game.
However, Purley were alive to this possibility. With determination from the
entire midfield in fighting for ground balls, and Matt Payne gaining most
of the face-off possession, the result was never in doubt. Final score
21 - 4.

Goals: Matous Hilar 5, Chris Spence 4, Sam Bugeja 4, Jamie Tasko 3, Matt
Payne 3, Mike Husey 1, Stuart Green 1

---
title: Bath Away
titleShort: 9 Nov Bath
date: 2002-11-09
category: match-report
---

Purley made steady, rather than spectacular, progress against Bath with
quarter time scores of 4-0, 8-0 and 13-0. Bath had squandered several
excellent scoring opportunities, but got their just rewards in the final
quarter, with Nick Roberts bagging both of their goals. But at the other
end Purley were having their best quarter, with several sweet passing moves
which left the final shot into an empty net. Final score 20-2.

With Purley's two top attack men taking a week off, the midfield needed to
take on the scoring responsibilities - and both Sam Bugeja and Matt Payne
obliged, with tallies of 8 and 4 goals respectively. Even the defence
helped out, as Andy Booth scored after a nice change of hands. In attack
new recruit Matous Hilar, from the Czech Republic, fitted into the side
nicely, and scored 2 goals on his debut.

Goals: Sam Bugeja 8, Matt Payne 4, Darren Novell 2, Matous Hilar 2, Andy
Booth 1, Stuart Green 1, Mike Husey 1, Mike Barrett 1

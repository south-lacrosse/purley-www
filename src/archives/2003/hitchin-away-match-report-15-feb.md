---
title: Hitchin Away
titleShort: 15 Feb Hitchin
date: 2003-02-15
category: match-report
---

With both teams only having 10 players, and no ref available, it was
decided to play the game without a referee. This can sometimes lead to
tension between the two sides, but on this occaision both teams acted in a
very sportsman like manner - it was a very clean game, but when there was a
foul both sides sent their own players to the sin bin, even when the other
team would probably have let the foul go.

The Purley attack were in their usual majestic form, and with 9-1 first
quarter scoreline the game was effectively over. Purley continued their
domination in the second quarter to take the score to 18-1, but Hitchin
rallied in the third only losing the quarter 3-6, and it looked like they
might keep the Purple and Gold below the thirty mark, but Purley managed
that with only seconds to go. Final score 30-4.

---
title: Hillcroft Away
titleShort: 15 Mar Hillcroft
date: 2003-03-15
category: match-report
---

Hillcroft are a hard-hitting and competitive side, but with only one
serious attacking option goals were hard to come by. Despite not being on
top form, the Purple and Gold managed to pull out a comfortable 23-5
victory - with Sam Bugeja showing the way with 9 goals.

Goals: Sam Bugeja 9, Tim Richmond 7, Darren Novell 3, Matt Payne 2, Mike
Husey 1, Ryan Lynch 1

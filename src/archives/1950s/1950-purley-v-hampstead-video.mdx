This game, played at Lord's, comes courtesy of [British
Pathé](https://www.britishpathe.com/). Purley are in the diagonal stripes. For those
unfamiliar of the game in those days, the game was 12-a-side, with no
boundaries, and no helmets! The result was a 4-4 draw.
